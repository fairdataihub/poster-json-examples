## Crossref

Crossref lets organizations that publisher research register identifiers and metadata for their content. This metadata can contain ORCID iDs, funding and licence information and more. This information is made freely available so that it can be integrated into their research information systems to track research outputs.

## ORCID

ORCID provides a persistent digital identifier that distinguishes each researcher from other researchers and, through integration in key workflows such as manuscript and grant submission, supports automated linkages between them and their professional activities ensuring that their work is recognised.
